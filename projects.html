Find Numbers

Objectives:

To identify the numbers that should be added together in order to achieve the target number from a provided array of numbers.

This task may look trivial but it is far from it.

Recently a family member who works in a taxation evironment asked me if I can make some program to solve this task faster than just doing it manually.

A payment was made and needed to identify which invoice added up the payment amount. This was the target number -3064559.81

Quick example:

Let say you have given a series of numbers like: 272224.15, 9226.38, 218133.12, 13319.99, -29235, -235571.8, -86565, -86485, -59325, -28957, -4255, -97282.81 -40067, -86071, -70332, -12707, 31212, 44185.37, -19381, -200072, -20641 (imagine you have dozens more), and you want to find out what numbers you need to add up to find the sum -306459.81. Not very easy right?
